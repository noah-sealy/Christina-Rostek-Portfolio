About Me.

Dear Top Hat, I want to express my interest in the National Account Executive position at Top Hat. With over 20 years of higher educational sales experience, I have built a career by winning large adoptions in my territory. I have a laser-focused ability to sort through the weeds, find the right people, tell the right stories, and get the business. For example, I was able to get an intro psychology adoption at a competitive author's school, which is virtually unheard of. My success in the field has been consistently noticed by the competition. For example, when I was at Maxwell MacMillan and it was being bought by what is now known as Pearson Education, I was one of only two employees who were asked to join Pearson. While at Pearson, Harcourt Brace approached me for a position in Atlantic Canada. While at Harcourt, Nelson approached me and hired me on the spot. At Nelson, I have over 20 years of successful sales experience, including institutional sales where I met with deans and curriculum consultants. I am one of only two sales reps at Nelson (now Cengage) who have the title Executive Learning Consultant. Only reps who consistently make their goal achieve this title. I am a generalist rep and sell into all departments of every school in my territory, which gives me a better understanding of the needs of the entire school, from the computing department to MBA program. In terms of content, I introduced Nelson to a professor who would go on to write one of our top selling psychology texts, which is now in its sixth edition along with several other titles. To Top Hat, I bring a wealth of knowledge about faculty needs and the competitive landscape and look forward to discussing my qualifications with you at your convenience. Sincerely, Christina Rostek
